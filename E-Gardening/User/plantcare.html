Step by Step

Easy plant care instructions.

  • Choose the right plant
  • Use quality soil
  • Plant in suitable pot
  • Water regularly

Sunlight & Water

Proper sunlight & watering.

  • Indoor: Low sunlight
  • Outdoor: Direct sunlight
  • Water morning or evening
🪴

Common Problems

Plant issues & solutions.

  • Yellow leaves → Less water
  • Dry leaves → More water
  • Pests → Natural pesticides
✅

Do's & Don'ts

Healthy plant habits.

  • Check soil moisture
  • Remove dry leaves
  • Avoid overwatering
