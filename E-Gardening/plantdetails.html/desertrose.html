Desert Rose Plant

🌺 Desert Rose Plant

Scientific Name: Adenium obesum

Common Names: Desert Rose, Mock Azalea, Impala Lily

Description: Desert Rose is a beautiful succulent flowering plant known for its thick swollen trunk (caudex) and vibrant trumpet-shaped flowers. It is commonly grown in pots as an ornamental plant.

Uses: Used mainly as an ornamental and bonsai-style plant. Adds beauty to balconies, gardens, and indoor sunny spaces.

Disadvantages: All parts of the plant are toxic if ingested. Sap may cause skin irritation. Not safe for pets and children.

Weather: Prefers hot and dry climates. Ideal temperature range is 20°C to 35°C. Cannot tolerate frost.

Soil: Requires well-drained sandy or succulent soil. Poor drainage can cause root rot.

Regular Watering: Water sparingly. Allow soil to dry completely between watering. Reduce watering during winter.

Sunlight: Requires full sunlight for healthy growth and flowering. At least 6–8 hours of sunlight daily.
