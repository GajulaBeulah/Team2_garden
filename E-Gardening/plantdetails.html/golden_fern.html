Golden Fern Plant

🌿 Golden Fern Plant

Scientific Name: Polystichum spp.

Common Names: Golden Fern, Shield Fern

Description: Golden Fern is a beautiful ornamental fern known for its golden-green fronds that add elegance to indoor and outdoor spaces. It is commonly used in home décor, offices, and shaded gardens.

Uses: Enhances indoor and outdoor aesthetics. Improves air quality and adds a fresh natural look. Widely used as a decorative houseplant.

Disadvantages: Sensitive to direct sunlight. Requires regular moisture and humidity. Overwatering may cause root rot.

Weather: Prefers cool to warm climates. Ideal temperature range is 15°C to 28°C.

Soil: Requires well-drained, moist, and organic-rich soil. A mix of garden soil, compost, and cocopeat works well.

Regular Watering: Water regularly to keep soil slightly moist. Avoid water stagnation.

Sunlight: Grows best in indirect sunlight or partial shade. Direct sunlight may scorch the fronds.
