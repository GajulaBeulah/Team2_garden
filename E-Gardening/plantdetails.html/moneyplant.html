Air Purifier Money Plant

🌱Air Purifier Money Plant

Scientific Name: Epipremnum aureum

Common Names: Money Plant, Devil’s Ivy, Golden Pothos

Description: The Money Plant is a popular indoor climbing plant known for its heart-shaped, glossy green leaves. It grows easily in water or soil and is widely used in homes and offices for its attractive appearance and low maintenance nature.

Uses: Helps improve indoor air quality by reducing pollutants, enhances home décor, promotes a calm and positive environment, and is believed to bring good luck and prosperity according to Vastu and Feng Shui.

Disadvantages: The plant is mildly toxic if leaves are consumed, especially for pets and small children. Overwatering can cause yellow leaves and root rot.

Weather: Prefers warm and humid climates. Ideal temperature range is 18°C to 30°C. Sensitive to extreme cold conditions.

Soil: Requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil. A mix of garden soil, sand, and compost works best. Can also grow well in water.

Regular Watering: Water once the top layer of soil becomes dry. Avoid overwatering. If grown in water, change the water every 7–10 days.

Sunlight: Grows best in bright, indirect sunlight. Can tolerate low light, but direct harsh sunlight may burn the leaves.
