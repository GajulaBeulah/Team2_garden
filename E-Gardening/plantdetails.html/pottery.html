Garden Pottery

🪴 Pottery (Garden Pots)

Types: Clay Pots, Terracotta Pots, Ceramic Pots, Earthen Pots

Description: Pottery refers to containers made from clay and fired at high temperatures. Garden pottery is commonly used for planting flowers, herbs, and small trees. These pots are eco-friendly and enhance the beauty of gardens and homes.

Uses: Used for growing indoor and outdoor plants. Helps regulate soil moisture and provides good air circulation to roots. Adds a traditional and aesthetic look to gardens and balconies.

Advantages: Natural and eco-friendly material. Allows better air and water flow. Prevents root rot and keeps plants healthy.

Disadvantages: Fragile and can break easily. Heavier than plastic pots. Dries out faster and needs frequent watering.

Weather: Suitable for all weather conditions. May crack in extreme cold or frost.

Maintenance: Clean pots regularly to avoid algae and salt buildup. Handle carefully to prevent breakage.

Best For: Succulents, Herbs, Flowering plants, Bonsai
