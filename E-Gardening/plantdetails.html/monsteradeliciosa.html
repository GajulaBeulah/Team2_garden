Monstera Deliciosa

🌿 Monstera Deliciosa

Scientific Name: Monstera deliciosa

Common Names: Swiss Cheese Plant, Split-leaf Philodendron

Description: Monstera Deliciosa is a popular tropical indoor plant known for its large, glossy green leaves with natural holes and splits. It is widely used in home décor for its bold and modern appearance.

Uses: Enhances interior décor and indoor aesthetics. Helps improve mood and creates a calming environment. Grown mainly as an ornamental houseplant.

Disadvantages: Toxic if ingested by pets or small children. Requires space as it grows large.

Weather: Prefers warm and humid climates. Ideal temperature range is 18°C to 30°C.

Soil: Requires well-drained, airy soil. A mix of potting soil, cocopeat, and perlite works well.

Regular Watering: Water when the top layer of soil feels dry. Avoid overwatering to prevent root rot.

Sunlight: Grows best in bright, indirect sunlight. Direct sunlight may scorch the leaves.
